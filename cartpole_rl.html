Inside the CartPole Simulation

What is the CartPole Environment?

The goal is to keep the red inverted pendulum balancing upright by pushing the blue cart either Left (-10N) or Right (+10N) along a frictionless track.

An episode ends (fails) if the pole tilts beyond ±12° or the cart moves off the edge (±2.4 units). The agent earns +1 reward for every time step the pole remains upright!

Interpreting the Policy Network SVG

The graph on the right panel visualizes the AI's "brain" as a two-layer Multi-Layer Perceptron (MLP):

  • Input Layer (4 Nodes): Receives the continuous physics variables [x, ẋ, θ, θ̇].
  • Hidden Nodes: Non-linear tanh activation nodes that combine state signals to recognize patterns (e.g., "falling right while moving fast").
  • Output Layer (2 Nodes): Softmax probabilities for deciding whether to push Left or Right.

Color & Weight Legend

Green Lines (Excitatory): Positive connection weight. Activates the destination neuron when signal is strong.
Red Lines (Inhibitory): Negative connection weight. Suppresses the destination neuron.
Thickness: Proportional to absolute magnitude (|W|). Thicker lines dominate the decision making!

How REINFORCE Learns

REINFORCE is a Monte Carlo policy gradient algorithm that directly optimizes the neural network weights via gradient ascent:

1
Rollout Episode: The cart interacts with the physics world until it falls or reaches 500 steps.
2
Discounted Return: Rewards are accumulated backward in time using discount factor Gamma (γ) so earlier actions receive credit for long survival.
3
Policy Update: Actions that led to high rewards are reinforced by tweaking network weights in the direction of policy gradients.

How the Genetic Algorithm Learns

Instead of calculating gradients, the Genetic Algorithm evolves a population of independent candidate networks simultaneously:

1
Parallel Population: Notice the multi-colored ghost carts! Each cart is controlled by a distinct neural network variant.
2
Survival of the Fittest: When all carts finish, the agent with the highest return (yellow highlight) is selected as champion.
3
Mutation (σ): Offspring networks are spawned by perturbing the champion's weights with Gaussian noise (controlled by Mutation σ slider).

Training Mode (Stochastic Exploration)

While training, the AI samples actions from its probability distribution. If P(Left) = 70%, it will still push Right 30% of the time!

Why? This randomness allows the agent to discover novel strategies and prevent getting stuck in suboptimal behavior.

Test Mode (Greedy Exploitation)

When you toggle Test Mode in the controls sidebar, exploration is disabled.

The cart strictly picks the action with the highest probability (argmax). Try flipping on Test Mode after a few episodes of training to watch the pure learned behavior without random mistakes!
